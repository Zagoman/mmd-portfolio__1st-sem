the experience you should expect

This portfolio has the goal of showing my whole first semester experience, and along with that, it is to show how I came up with solutions to the assignments. You should expect to have a resumed explanation of what the goals of each assignment were, and also to have a step-by step demonstration on my process to solve them. Along with my solutions to each assignment, there will be insights of what I learnt and what I felt it was important to share. The assignments are divided into themes, so each portfolio piece is going to be representing one theme, and inside this page, there is going to be all assignments related to that specific theme. The pages are going to be long, so feel free to take a break, drink some coffee or tea, and come back to it… it is worth it. Finally, you have the chance to learn a bit more about me, and hopefully have an understanding of my personality and ambitions, you might be surprised on the way, or maybe get some answers for questions you’ve never had. Enjoy your experience.
